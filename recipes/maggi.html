Maggi

Description

As an indian who does not just love maggi, it takes just 2 minutes to prepare(atleast it is supposed to) and is a delight.Today we will be teaching you a recipe to spicy vegitable maggi, which will make you maggi both tasty and healthy.

Ingredients

  • Maggi noodles
  • Vegitalbes: Onion, tomatos, peas, carots, any other vegitables of your choice
  • Spices: Red chilli, turmeric powder, corriander powder
  • Salt
  • Cooling Oil(of your choice)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut your Vegitalbes
  2. Put 2 tablespoons of cooking oil on the stove on high flame.
  3. After oil is hot, add half onion and stir properly.
  4. When onion turns brownish add maggi masala and other masala as per your choice, Stir well.
  5. Add the remaining vegitable and cook them for 2 minute
  6. Add 250 ml water and 1 packet maggi noodles.
  7. Add salt as per your taste.
